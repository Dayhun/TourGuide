SUMMARY

Brazil is a country of continental dimensions with an area of more than 8.5 million km², located in South America, with capital in Brasilia. It presents an enormous landscape, economic and cultural diversity.

The Brazilian population is quite mixed. This occurred due to the mixing of different human groups that happened in the country. There are countless races that favored the formation of the Brazilian people. The main groups were indigenous peoples, Africans, European and Asian immigrants. The Brazilian people are known for their hospitality, joy and love of music and dance. With a rich history, traditions and customs. In addition, Brazil is rich in natural resources, which makes it an important country on the global stage.
